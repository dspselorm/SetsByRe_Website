About Us

At SetsByRe, we bring creative visions to life through thoughtful and immersive set design. From intimate celebrations to large-scale productions, we specialize in transforming spaces into visually captivating experiences that leave lasting impressions.

Some of our key services include:

  • Indoor Set Design (Interior Decor)
  • Outdoor Set Design (Exterior Decor)
  • Event Planning
  • Backdrop Design and Setup

Our work blends creativity, detail, and functionality to create environments that reflect each client’s unique story and purpose. Whether it’s weddings, corporate events, stage productions, photoshoots, or brand activations, we approach every project with originality and precision.

We believe that every space has the potential to inspire. That’s why we focus on creating designs that are not only visually striking, but also memorable, elegant, and tailored to each occasion.

At SetsByRe, design is more than decoration — it’s an experience.

01 What services does SetsByRe offer?

We specialize in creative set design and event styling for weddings, corporate events, stage productions, photoshoots, brand activations, and special occasions.

02 How early should I book your services?

We recommend booking as early as possible, especially during peak event seasons, to ensure availability and enough time for planning and execution.

03 Do you work outside your location?

Yes, depending on the project requirements and logistics, we are available to work in different locations.

04 Can I request a consultation before booking?

Absolutely. We offer consultations to discuss your ideas, event goals, budget, and design preferences.

05 What is included in your set design service?

Our services may include concept development, backdrop design, stage styling, décor setup, lighting coordination, and overall visual arrangement depending on the project.

06 How can I get a quote?

You can contact us through the website’s contact section or social media platforms with details about your event, and we’ll provide a customized quote.

07 Do you provide setup and breakdown services?

Yes, our team handles both installation and dismantling to ensure a smooth and stress-free experience for clients.

08 Can I see examples of your previous work?

Yes. Our portfolio section showcases selected projects, photos, and videos from previous events and productions.
